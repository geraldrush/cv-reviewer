- Analyze as many CVs as you want

### For PREMIUM Users:
**"Transform Your CV for One-Time Investment"**
- Understand exactly how ATS systems see your CV
- Know what recruiters are looking for
- Get precise keyword alignment metrics
- Optimize formatting and presentation
- Use AI to rewrite and improve instantly
- Export professional ATS-optimized PDFs
- Industry-standard best practices applied
- Competitive advantage in your job search

## Implementation Details

### Free Tier Display:
- Hides: ATS tabs, Recruiter tabs, Keyword tabs, Format cards
- Shows: Single score, reasoning, 3-5 improvements
- CTA: "Upgrade to see all 4+ metrics"

### Premium Tier Display:
- Shows: All 4 metric cards, detailed tabs, full analysis
- Actions: AI rewrite, apply improvements, export PDF
- No limitations or upgrade prompts

### Data Management:
- Both tiers run FULL backend analysis
- Premium tiers display all results
- Free tiers see curated results
- Cost: No extra backend computation needed
